The beginning

Every great love starts simply

✦  ·  ✦

Ours began with a moment that felt completely ordinary — a conversation that stretched well past its natural ending, laughter that surprised us both, and a quiet realisation that something had quietly, unmistakably shifted.

What followed was not a fairytale — it was something better. It was real: patience and honesty, growth and trust, a thousand small choosing-each-other moments that built, slowly, into a life we couldn't imagine without the other.

The first meeting

A conversation that didn't end

We met the way most meaningful things happen — unexpectedly, in an ordinary moment that turned out to be anything but. One conversation turned into many more. We were both trying not to make it obvious how much we enjoyed each other's company.

✉️

Early days

Getting to know each other

Late nights talking about everything and nothing. Slowly discovering how much we had in common — and how much we didn't — and finding that both felt like gifts. The world got a little bigger every time we were together.

🌿

Growing together

Choosing each other, every day

Love, we found, is not one large moment. It's a thousand small ones — a cup of tea made just right, showing up on a hard day, choosing patience when it would have been easier not to. We kept choosing.

💍

The proposal

She said yes

The question had been living in Onkgopotse's heart for some time before the words finally arrived. When they did, there was no doubt — Pabalelo said yes before the sentence was finished. Now we're here, and we couldn't be more ready.
