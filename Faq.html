RSVP & Invitations

When is the RSVP deadline? +

We kindly ask that you RSVP by 1 November 2025 so we can finalise numbers with the venue and catering team. You can RSVP directly on our RSVP page.

Can I bring a plus one? +

Please check your invitation carefully — it will reflect exactly how many guests are included. Due to venue capacity, we're unable to accommodate additional guests beyond what's on your invite.

Are children welcome at the wedding? +

We love your little ones dearly! Children named on your invitation are absolutely welcome to join us. If you're unsure whether your children are included, please reach out to us directly.

I need to change my RSVP — what do I do? +

Plans change — we understand. Simply re-submit your RSVP through our form and leave a note in the message field, or contact us directly so we can update our records.

Dress Code & Attire

What is the dress code? +

We'd love for guests to dress in formal or semi-formal attire. Think elegant — it's a celebration and we want you to feel as beautiful as the day itself. Our colour palette is sage green and taupe, so feel free to complement it if you'd like.

Is there anything I should avoid wearing? +

We kindly ask guests not to wear white or ivory — reserved for the bride! Casual attire like jeans or shorts is also not in keeping with the day's tone. Outside of that, come dressed to celebrate.

What shoes should I wear? Is there a lawn? +

Part of the event takes place outdoors during the cocktail hour. If you plan to wear heels, a chunkier heel or wedge may be more comfortable on grassed areas. Full venue details will be shared closer to the day.

On the Day

What time should I arrive? +

Please aim to be seated by 11:50 so the ceremony can begin on time at 12:00. Doors open at 11:30. We'd hate for anyone to miss the ceremony!

Is parking available at the venue? +

Yes — on-site parking is available. Full venue details including directions and parking information will be shared with all confirmed guests ahead of the day.

Can I take photos during the ceremony? +

We have a professional photographer capturing every moment, so we're asking guests to keep their phones away during the ceremony itself — we want to see your faces, not your screens! You're absolutely welcome to take photos at the reception and throughout the rest of the day.

Will there be transport between the ceremony and reception? +

The ceremony and reception are taking place at the same venue, so no transport is needed between them. If you need help with travel arrangements, please reach out to us.

Food & Drink

How do I let you know about dietary requirements? +

Please include your dietary requirements when you submit your RSVP — there's a dedicated field on the form. We'll make sure the catering team is fully briefed. If you have a severe allergy, please also contact us directly.

Is there an open bar? +

Yes! Drinks are included throughout the day — from welcome drinks during cocktail hour to wine and soft drinks at the reception. We've made sure there's plenty for everyone.
