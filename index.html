Q & A

Good to know

✦  ·  ✦

What should I wear?

Formal or semi-formal attire. Dress to celebrate — this is a beautiful occasion and we'd love for you to feel the part.

Can I bring a plus one?

Please check your invitation — it will indicate whether a guest is included. Space is limited, so kindly RSVP for the exact number on your invite.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones! If children are included on your invitation, they're warmly welcome to join us.

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please RSVP via our Joy website as soon as possible so we can plan ahead. Check the RSVP section for the exact deadline.

Do you have dietary options?

Yes — please note any dietary requirements when you RSVP and we'll make sure you're well taken care of.
